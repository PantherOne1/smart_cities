Partners

The Smart Cities Organization is partnered with multiple cities all around the Philippines who will be the pioneers of the smart evolution in the country. Cities like Manila, Davao, Cebu, Gensan, and Zamboanga are intending to transform their ways for good for the people.

We are also partnered with various private sectors, such as Privately Owned Financing Corporations and Transportation Corporations.

Vision

The Smart Cities Organization envisions a country where people can do their daily activities with more convenience, letting them enjoy the moment. By 2040, the Smart Cities movement ensures that major cities inside the country have completed their evolution towards a more secure, sound, smart city.

Mission

The Smart Cities Organization is devoted to connecting different sectors to hasten the development of cities that wish to reach the goal of the movement. We are passionate about making the Philippines one of the developing countries that give importance to technologies as the way to reach a better environment for the population.

We will ensure that the movement will correlate to the development of the cities’ capabilities with the help of nurturing their connection with technologies.
